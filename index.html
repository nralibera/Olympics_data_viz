Welcome to the World Athlete Map !

This map displays athletes journey through their paticipation in Olympic games (Summer and Winter).

Athlete Selection

To discover the achievements of your favorite athlete, you can use the search bar and click on the name when it is suggested.

You can select multiple athletes to draw their journey at the same time. The selected ones will be displayed next to the search bar and a little click on the name will make it disapear from selection.

Athlete Journey

Click on the path itself to get more details about the athletes and their results. Or on the legend if displayed (Only displayed till 15 athletes)

Database

Our database hosts 155.029 athletes from the first modern games in 1896 to 2022. So hopefully, your favorite one is among them.

Country Selection

More intersted in country result ? Feel free to directly click on one of them on the map. You will discover all the nationals who participated in at least the olympic games of the year shown at the top right of the map (You can also change it: if not a games year, closest year will be calculated)
